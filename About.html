HISTORY OF THE BUSINESS

Richway Builders was first established in 1993. It was first owned by the parents of the current owner, Engr. Richard Plata, who invested their savings to instigate the business located in Cainta Floodway. His uncle, Boy Plata, was the first one to manage the business and was able to employ a handful of people.

Being the eldest among his siblings, his parents encouraged him to be the one to manage and operate the business, but he declined the offer. At that time, Mr. Plata was working as a Junior Project Engineer in a construction project management company located in Makati City.

Five years later, Mr. Plata came up with a decision to finally acquire the business established by his parents. He realized that being self-employed and having your own business enable you to have more time for yourself and your family, as well as having financial freedom.

By the following year, 2023, marks Richway Builders Supply's 30th year in the construction supply industry.

Engr. Richard Plata - Current Owner
